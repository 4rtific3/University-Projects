Napoli Pizza Village

In Napoli, otherwise Naples, Italy is the world’s largest Pizzafest (the festival of pizza) celebrated each year. There are thousands of pizza lovers visiting Naples for the Pizzafest, to try the best, most famous pizzas’ from Naples, Italy. If you’re wondering what to do next year, and you love pizza as much as we do, you should definitely consider a trip to Naples during this delicious festival.

Naples holds this event in honor of pizza every year: for 5 to 11 days. The most famous pizzerie from all over the world, compete against renowned local pizzaioli, who set up stands with tables where people can sit and taste different varieties of pizzas. At the end of the week-long celebration, a jury will judge, also by taking into account the opinion of all those who ate at the Pizzafest, the various pizzas and proclaim the best pizzaiolo in the world.

Fiera Creattiva Napoli

Creattiva is the National Fair of Manual Arts, a Business to Consumer event in which more than 300 companies meet thousands of hobbyists passionate about creative manual arts. Creattiva's exhibitors, who come from all regions of Italy and also from abroad, promote techniques to give shape to creativity through hundreds of courses and demonstrations; they also commercialize all the products necessary for the realization of many creative ideas: diagrams, kits and semi-finished products are the masters!

Schools, institutes and associations from all over Italy are ready to reveal the secrets to visitors and to exhibit the best works of the art of embroidery. In addition to the exhibition, dozens of courses and demonstrations will be organized during the three days of the fair, allowing the public to steal the secrets of this art directly from the experts, bearers of traditions and manual skills.

Courses and demonstrations have always been one of Creattiva's flagships: they allow visitors to experience first-hand the birth of real creative masterpieces, sometimes starting from simple loose materials. The activities are divided into courses, during which the visitors themselves carry out the projects proposed by the exhibitors, and demonstrations, faster activities during which users watch the execution by the experts.
